Welcome to CSE and ET department


Welcome to the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) and Electronics and Telecommunications (ET). Our department is dedicated to fostering innovation, technical excellence, and cutting-edge research in the fields of computer science and telecommunications. With a team of highly qualified faculty and state-of-the-art facilities, we strive to provide students with a comprehensive education that blends theoretical knowledge with practical applications. Our programs are designed to prepare graduates for successful careers in industry and academia, empowering them with the skills to solve complex problems and drive technological advancements.
