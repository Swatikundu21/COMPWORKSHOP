About Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) and Emerging Technologies (ET) is committed to excellence in teaching, research, and innovation. Our programs are designed to equip students with the necessary skills to thrive in the ever-changing technological landscape.

The Department of CSE and ET offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs that focus on building a solid foundation in core computing principles, as well as emerging areas such as Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and Cybersecurity.
